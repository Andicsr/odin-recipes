Guacamole

Typical mexican avocado dish

Guacamole is an easy dish to make because it has basic ingredients such as onion, tomato, and lime. However, there is not just one recipe, you can find several types of guacamole, but I assure you that you will love this one!

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • Cilantro
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 jalapeno (optional)
  • Lime Juice
  • Salt
  • 1 tomato
  • Tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Chop the onion, tomato and jalapeno.
  2. Cut the cilantro leaves as small as you can.
  3. Slice three ripe avocados in half, remove the pit and scoop them into a mixing bowl.
  4. Then use a fork to gently mash them to your desired level of chunky or smooth.
  5. Add the other ingredients (cilantro, onion, jalapeno, lime juice and tomato).
  6. Incorporate the Ingredients and salt to taste
  7. Finally, add tortilla chips. And enjoy it!

Lemon Pie

Delicious dessert made with 7 simple ingredients

Dessert internationally famous for its flavor and texture, it can be prepared with advanced techniques or made more accessible to any kitchen.

Ingredients

  • About 160g of full-sheet graham crackers
  • 62g of salted almonds
  • 2 Tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 5 Tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cans of full-fat sweetened condensed milk
  • Fresh lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
  • 4 large egg yolks

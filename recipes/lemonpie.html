Lemon Pie

Delicious dessert made with 7 simple ingredients

Dessert internationally famous for its flavor and texture, it can be prepared with advanced techniques or made more accessible to any kitchen.

Ingredients

  • About 160g of full-sheet graham crackers
  • 62g of salted almonds
  • 2 Tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 5 Tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cans of full-fat sweetened condensed milk
  • Fresh lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
  • 4 large egg yolks

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (177°C).
  2. Using a food processor, pulse the graham crackers and almonds together into crumbs. Pour into a medium bowl and stir in the sugar. Add the melted butter and stir until combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ungreased 9-inch pie dish. With medium pressure using your hand, pat the crumbs down into the bottom and up the sides to make a compact crust.
  4. Pre-bake crust for 8 minutes. Remove from the oven and leave the oven on.
  5. Whisk the sweetened condensed milk, lemon juice, and egg yolks together. Pour into warm crust.
  6. Bake the pie for 19/21 minutes or until only slightly jiggly in the center. Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely on a wire rack.
  7. Once cool, cover and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
  8. Garnish as desired. And enjoy it!
